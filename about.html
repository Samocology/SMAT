Our Story

SMAT was founded in 2015 with a unique vision to combine three distinct services under one roof: a printing press, a gym, and a flower shop. What started as a small business has grown into a thriving enterprise serving thousands of customers across Nigeria.

Our founder, Mr. Samuel Adeyemi, noticed a gap in the market for a one-stop solution that could cater to both business and personal needs. He envisioned a place where professionals could print their business materials, maintain their physical health, and purchase beautiful flowers for special occasions - all in one convenient location.

Over the years, we have expanded our services and facilities to meet the growing demands of our customers. Today, SMAT is recognized as a leader in all three industries, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.
