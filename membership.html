Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership program

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership by visiting our facility in person or by sending an email to membership@smat.com. Please note that we require a 30-day notice for all cancellations.

Can I freeze my membership temporarily?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. There is a small administrative fee for this service. Please contact our membership team for more details.

Are there any joining fees?

Yes, there is a one-time joining fee of ₦5,000 for all new members. This fee covers your welcome package and administrative costs.

Can I upgrade my membership plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership plan at any time. The new rate will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.
