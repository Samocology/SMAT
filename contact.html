Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your business hours?

Our business hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. The gym is accessible 24/7 for members with their access cards.

Do you offer corporate services?

Yes, we offer corporate services for printing, gym memberships, and flower arrangements. We can create custom packages tailored to your company's needs. Please contact our corporate sales team for more information.

How can I provide feedback about your services?

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through our contact form on this page, by emailing godstimesml@gmail.com, or by speaking with any of our staff members in person.

Do you deliver flowers?

Yes, we offer flower delivery services within Lagos and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location and urgency. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 1:00 PM.
